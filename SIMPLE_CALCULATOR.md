# Simple Pricing Calculator

## What It Is
A dead-simple 2-step pricing calculator. No complexity, no modes, no confusion.

## How It Works

**Step 1:** Pick your aircraft type
- Light Aircraft (C172, C182, Archer)
- High Performance (SR22, Mooney, Bonanza)  
- Turbine (TBM, Vision Jet)

**Step 2:** Pick your monthly hours
- 0-10 hrs
- 10-20 hrs
- 20-30 hrs
- 30+ hrs

**Result:** Instant price + "Get This Quote" button
